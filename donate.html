Ways to Donate

Whole Blood

The most common type of donation where approximately one pint of blood is drawn.

  • Duration: 45-60 minutes
  • Can donate every 56 days
  • Most flexible donation type
Learn More

Power Red

A donation that collects concentrated red blood cells using apheresis technology.

  • Duration: 1.5 hours
  • Can donate every 112 days
  • Ideal for O-, O+, A-, B- donors
Learn More

Plasma

Plasma donation through apheresis collects the liquid portion of your blood.

  • Duration: 1.5 hours
  • Can donate every 28 days
  • AB plasma is most needed
Learn More

Your Impact

When you donate blood, you're not just giving a resource—you're giving time. Time for a patient to recover, time for families to make memories, and time for medical teams to provide life-saving treatments.

Every two seconds, someone in the world needs blood. Your donation helps ensure that blood is available when it's needed most.
